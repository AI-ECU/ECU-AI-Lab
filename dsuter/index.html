Professor David Suter

I am currently a Research Professor in AI at Edith Cowan University, Perth, Western Australia.

I was a Professor of Computer Science at the University of Adelaide from 2008-2017. For some years I was also Head of School of Computer Science there. Prior to that (1992-2008) I held various positions at Monash University including Professor and Associate Dean Research (Faculty of Engineering)

My first appointment was a Lecturer at La Trobe University But that was so.....last century!

I was (until March 2021) an associate editor for the journal Pattern Recognition and I am a member of the Honourary Editorial Board of Int. J. of Computer Vision.
