Professor David Suter

I am currently a Research Professor in AI at Edith Cowan University, Perth, Western Australia.

I was a Professor of Computer Science at the University of Adelaide from 2008-2017. For some years I was also Head of School of Computer Science there. Prior to that (1992-2008) I held various positions at Monash University inculding Professor and Assoaciate Dean Research (Faculty of Engineering)

I am currently an associate editor for the journal Pattern Recognition and on the Honourary Editorial Board of Int. J. of Computer Vision.
